Sustainability Pillars

Three Dimensions of Our Sustainability

🌍
Environment (Planet)

Integrated organic farming without harmful chemical pesticides. Water-efficient production process with zero-waste output. Organic fertilizer from mocaf waste replaces chemical inputs.

🤝
People

Fair and stable purchasing prices for farmers. Continuous training and capacity-building programs. Women's empowerment and inclusivity throughout the value chain.

📈
Economy (Profit)

A shared-value business model where farmers, craftsmen, and the company grow together. Global market expansion increases the value of local Indonesian produce.

🌿
Organic Farming

Integrated Organic Farming

Our partner farmers apply integrated organic farming practices — using organic fertilizer derived from mocaf production waste, avoiding harmful chemical pesticides, and maintaining the balance of the farmland ecosystem.

This approach not only produces higher-quality cassava, but also preserves long-term soil fertility and the health of Banjarnegara's agricultural ecosystem for generations to come.

  • ✓ No harmful chemical pesticides
  • ✓ Organic fertilizer from mocaf waste
  • ✓ Crop rotation to maintain soil fertility
  • ✓ Efficient irrigation systems that conserve water
Fair Economy

Economic Justice for Farmers

One of the biggest challenges facing farmers is poor economic conditions caused by volatile prices and dependence on middlemen who often take an unfair cut. Rumah Mocaf was created to solve this problem.

We purchase cassava at fair, transparent prices that do not fluctuate with unpredictable market forces. Farmers receive income certainty that allows them to plan for the future — investing in education, health, and their farms.

"With Rumah Mocaf's fair pricing system, I can finally plan my children's school fees with confidence." — Partner Farmer, Banjarnegara

Looking Ahead

Our Forward Commitments

Our sustainability agenda for 2025–2027.

2025
Farmer Ecosystem Expansion

Target 1,000 partner farmers across Banjarnegara and surrounding areas. Expand the organic farming training program to new communities.

2026
Zero-Carbon Production Facility

Investment in renewable energy (solar panels) for the production facility. Target carbon-neutral operations for the primary production line.

2027
International Certification & Premium Export

Achieve international organic certifications (EU Organic, USDA Organic) to unlock premium export markets in Europe and North America.

UN SDGs
