Our Ecosystem

Four Pillars of
the Rumah Mocaf Ecosystem

A circular, sustainable chain — from cassava field to premium product to table.

🌾
Farmers
Sustainable Cassava Cultivation

At the heart of our ecosystem, farmers practice integrated organic farming — managing every step from planting high-quality seedlings to harvesting with sustainable care.

🏭
Craftsmen
Transforming Cassava into Premium Mocaf Flour

Craftsmen convert raw cassava into high-quality, gluten-free mocaf flour through meticulous stages — soaking, pressing, drying, and sieving — ensuring superior quality at every step.

🚀
Youth Innovators
Global Distribution and Innovation

Youth drive quality control, product certification, packaging, branding, and R&D — continuously innovating mocaf derivatives and expanding Rumah Mocaf's global reach.

♻️
Livestock Farmers
Circular Economy from Mocaf Waste

Livestock farmers convert mocaf production waste into animal feed and organic fertilizer — reducing environmental impact and creating added value through a true circular economy.
