About Us

We are an agri-food company born from the passion to empower local cassava farmers and deliver premium cassava-based products for Indonesia and the world.

History

From Banjarnegara, for Indonesia

Rumah Mocaf Indonesia was founded in 2017 in Banjarnegara, Central Java — a cassava-rich region where farmers still struggled with volatile prices and limited market access.

We started with one simple yet powerful vision: to build an ecosystem connecting farmers with consumers through high-quality mocaf products. From a single small processing unit, Rumah Mocaf has grown into a leading player in the Indonesian mocaf market.

Vision & Mission

Why We Exist

Vision

To become a globally recognized premium Indonesian mocaf brand — realizing sustainable cassava-based food security while empowering local communities.

Mission

To build an integrated cassava farming ecosystem, empower farmers through fair pricing, create internationally quality-standard innovative products, and contribute to the UN SDGs.

Approach

We believe change begins with community. Every business decision we make considers its impact on farmers, the environment, and future generations.

Core Values

The Values That Guide Us

Community Empowerment

Every step we take is oriented toward the well-being of farmers and local communities in Banjarnegara.

Sustainability

We practice integrated organic farming and circular economy principles that minimize environmental impact.

Premium Quality

Every product passes strict quality standards to ensure consumer satisfaction and trust in global markets.

Continuous Innovation

We continuously innovate mocaf-derived products that are relevant to the needs of today's health-conscious consumers.

Fairness & Inclusion

Our work system is performance-based, open to women, and provides equal opportunities to all partners regardless of background.

Global Impact

We bring local Indonesian products to the international stage through global exhibitions and premium export channels.

Our Journey

From 2017 to Today

2017
Founded in Banjarnegara

Rumah Mocaf Indonesia was established with modest capital but enormous drive to empower local cassava farmers.

2018
Building the Farmer Ecosystem

We began recruiting and training partner farmers, establishing a fair-price purchasing system that replaced exploitative middlemen.

2019
Product Certification & Standardization

Obtained halal and BPOM certifications. Standardized the production process to meet modern retail and export quality requirements.

2020
Product Expansion & Digital Presence

Launched the Mocafine product line and began digital sales. Thrived through the pandemic thanks to a resilient local ecosystem.

2021–2022
International Exhibition Debut

First international appearances at SIAL Interfood Indonesia and MIHAS Malaysia. Mocaf products attracted buyers from multiple countries.

2023
Hannover Messe & the European Market

Presented at Hannover Messe Germany, introducing mocaf to the European market. Achieved #3 national digital market share at ~27%.

2024–2025
Global Expansion & 625+ Farmers

Exhibited at FHA Singapore, Odicoff Turkiye, TEI 40th, and Amazing Indonesia Jeddah. Ecosystem now spans 625+ farmers, 265+ craftsmen, 124+ youth innovators.
